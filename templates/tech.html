Summer Camp

Smarctek is committed to providing students in grades 7-12 the opportunity to learn coding through our summer camp program. The summer camp will be 100% online, with the focus on preparing students with the fundamentals of project life cycles while offering a safe space for them to be active, make new friends and create lifelong memories. By attending the camp, students will have the opportunity to earn a digital certificate representing the skills and knowledge gained from reading, writing and compiling code while designing and developing web and mobile applications. Also giving them the ability to build their own custom apps, freelance or further expand their knowledge. We are here to provide them the support they need all the way!

Session – 2: August 3rd – August 24th |8am to 12pm EST M – F.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What if I miss a session?

    Missing a session can be very critical when attending a bootcamp because so much of work is packed in each one! In the case of missing a lecture, you can always view any of the recorded session in your profile.

  • Will there be any placement assistance?

    Students joined in the bootcamp will provide an opportunity to attend a mock- interview sessions provided weekly.

  • What are prerequisites for these courses?
